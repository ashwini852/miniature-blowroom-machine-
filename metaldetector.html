Metal Detector Machine

Metal Detector Machine

  1. Detection Process: The machine has an electromagnetic field generated by sensors. As fibers pass through the machine, any metal object disrupts this field, triggering an alarm or stopping the process.
  2. Separation Process: Once detected, an automatic reject mechanism (such as an air jet or mechanical separator) removes the contaminated portion. This ensures that only clean fibers proceed further, preventing damage to subsequent machinery like carding machines.
  3. Control System: The system includes a digital display and sensitivity settings. The operator can adjust sensitivity levels based on material type and required purity.

Original Metal Detector Machine Specifications

  • Width: 800 mm to 1500 mm
  • Height: 1000 mm to 2000 mm
  • Length: 1200 mm to 3000 mm
  • Conveyor Width: 600 mm to 1200 mm
  • Detection Tunnel Size: 600 mm × 800 mm to 1000 mm × 1500 mm (adjustable based on material flow)
