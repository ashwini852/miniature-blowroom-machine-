Model Details

The Miniaturized Blowroom Prototype is designed to demonstrate the fiber opening, cleaning, and blending processes essential in textile spinning. This model replicates industrial blowroom machinery on a laboratory scale, allowing students and researchers to observe and understand fiber processing efficiently.

Bale Opener

The Bale Opener is the first stage in the blowroom process, responsible for breaking down compressed fiber bales into smaller tufts for further processing. This machine ensures uniform fiber distribution, reducing stress on subsequent cleaning stages...

Metal Detector

The Blowroom Metal Detector prevents damage to downstream machinery by detecting and removing metal contaminants from raw fibers. This system uses electromagnetic sensors that trigger an alarm when metallic objects are detected.

Pre-Cleaner

The Pre-Cleaner plays a crucial role in removing large-sized trash particles and dust from fiber tufts. The machine employs double hooks and an optimized cleaning grid to ensure effective dedusting without damaging the fiber.
