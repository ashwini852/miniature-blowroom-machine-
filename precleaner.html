Pre-Cleaner Machine

Pre-Cleaner Machine

This prototype was developed to demonstrate the mechanical design, material flow, and dedusting principles of our full-scale cleaning system. Though not operational, the model visually explains the cleaning stages and routing system for material and waste. It's an essential tool for presentations, educational purposes.

Prototype Specifications

  1. Frame Size: 1' x 1' x 1' (L x B x H)
  2. Opening Roller: 2” diameter
  3. Cleaning Roller: 1” diameter

Material Used

To construct the prototype, we used lightweight, easily workable materials to ensure flexibility during the design process:

  • Foam Sheets: 3mm and 5mm thickness – for structure and panels
  • PVC Pipes: 2” and 1” diameter – to represent rollers and air pathways
  • PVC Pipe Curves - Simulated dust collection mechanism
  • Glue Gun & Glue Sticks - For strong, clean bonding
  • Bound Components - For reinforcement and assembly
