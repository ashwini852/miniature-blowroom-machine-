MODEL DETAILS

Multimixer Machine

Multimixer Machine

The main objectives of developing the Prototype Multi-Mixer are to provide a visual and structural representation of the final opening and cleaning stage in the blowroom process, to help students and visitors understand the working principles, layout, and key components of the original CL-C3 machine, and to demonstrate the flow of material, placement of beaters, and trash removal paths in a compact and accessible model.

The Prototype Multi-Mixer is a scaled-down, non-functional model created to demonstrate the structural and mechanical design of a CL-C3 stand-alone cleaner used in blowroom processes. Designed primarily for educational and conceptual understanding, this prototype mirrors the original machinery’s configuration while using lightweight and safe materials for ease of handling and cost-effectiveness.

Prototype Multi-Mixer Specifications

• Frame size: 2ft × 1ft × 1ft (L × B × H)
• Chamber Width: 4"
• Chamber Length: 9" + 4" below it
• Feed Box: 5" × 5"
• Take-up Roller Diameter: 1"
• Opening Roller Diameter: 1"

Materials Used

To ensure safety and ease of assembly, the model is built using accessible materials:

• Foam Sheet (3mm) – for padding and structural flexibility
• Transparent Acrylic Sheet (2mm) – for chamber visibility
• PVC Pipe – for mock roller and pipe assembly
• Non-woven Fabric – simulates fiber handling areas
• Bound & Glue-stick – used for firm assembly
