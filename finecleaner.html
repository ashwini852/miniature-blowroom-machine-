Fine-Cleaner Machine

The primary objective of developing this Prototype Fine Cleaner Machine is to provide a visual and structural representation of the final cleaning stage in the blowroom process. This model is designed to help students, visitors, and industry professionals understand the working principles, layout, and essential components of a Fine Cleaner machine — particularly the mechanisms used for detecting and separating foreign parts from cotton.

While advanced machines like the Foreign Part Separator SP-FPU by Truetzschler have set benchmarks in removing impurities this prototype serves an educational purpose.

Prototype Fine-Cleaner Specifications

• Frame Dimensions: 2ft × 1.5ft × 1.5ft (L × B × H)
• Feed Roller Diameter: 1”
• Opening Roller Diameter: 1”
• Grid Bar Width: 2”
• Trash Roller Diameter: 1”

Materials Used

• Foam Sheet (3mm & 5mm): For structural framework and padding
• Transparent Acrylic Sheet (2mm): To provide clear visibility of the inner chamber and material flow
• PVC Pipe: Simulates rollers and other cylindrical components
• Bound & Glue-stick: For firm and secure assembly
